Yumi.eco

The YUMI.ECO application works in the Tri-City area. For a fee of PLN 0.49 per minute of driving a vehicle, residents are supplied with a fleet of 40 electric scooters parked after passing through the zone set by the server application. The realtime database supports instant payment Tpay and PayPal, among others payment cards. Employees have an administration app available to oversee the fleet and keep the batteries charged.

Mobile apps are available on Google Play and the App Store. Electronics program the on-board design of the scooters, along with the designs of PCBs and subassemblies. We served approx. 3000 regular users. Currently, the system design and fleet has been resold.
